Introduction

Bharathi Dairy is a milk delivery and subscription service. We built this app so our customers can place orders, manage subscriptions, and stay on top of their daily dairy needs — simply and reliably.

We understand that sharing personal information requires trust. This Privacy Policy explains in plain language what data we collect when you use our app, why we collect it, and exactly how it is used. Nothing is collected beyond what is described here.

By creating an account and using the Bharathi Dairy app, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have any questions, you are always welcome to reach out to us directly.

03

How We Use Your Information

Every piece of data we collect has a specific, legitimate purpose. We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not build profiles to share with marketers.

Order Processing
Your name, address, and phone number are used to prepare and dispatch your daily dairy orders accurately.
Delivery Management
Your delivery address is shared with our delivery team solely to ensure your products arrive at the right location.
Customer Support
We use your contact details to respond to queries, resolve complaints, and handle order-related issues.
Order Notifications
We April send order confirmations and delivery updates via WhatsApp or SMS to your registered phone number.

We do not share your information with any third party except as described in this policy. Your data is never sold or rented.

05

Data Security

We take reasonable and practical measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

  • Passwords are stored using one-way hashing. We never store your password in plain text and have no ability to read it.
  • All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS and TLS protocols.
  • Access to user data within our team is strictly limited to staff who require it to operate the service.
  • We do not store any payment information. Financial transactions, where applicable, are handled by secure third-party payment gateways.

While we take every reasonable precaution, no system is entirely immune to risk. In the event of a security breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you promptly.

Your Rights

Your data belongs to you. At any point, you have the right to take the following actions regarding your personal information.

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction — Ask us to correct any information that is inaccurate or out of date.
  • Deletion — Request that we delete your account and all associated personal data. We will process this within 7 business days.
  • Opt-out — Unsubscribe from promotional or notification messages at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.
  • Portability — Request your data in a readable, portable format if you wish to take it elsewhere.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in the section below. We will respond within a few business days.
